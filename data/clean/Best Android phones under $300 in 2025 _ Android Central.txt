Nothing's budget sub-brand CMF hit it out of the park with its

MediaTek chipset, Nothing has done a great job in terms of

The cameras on the CMF Phone 2 Pro are pretty great as well, for
the segment. Daylight shots come packed with good details and
vibrancy, and even low-light stills deliver good colors with no
visible noise. And of course, the Nothing OS is clean and has a
good level of customizability. The phone even has the dedicated
Essential Key that debuted with the Nothing Phone 3a Pro, and it
allows you to quickly access the Essential Space dashboard.
