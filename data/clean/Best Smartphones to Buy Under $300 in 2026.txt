The Motorola Moto G Power 5G (2024) is the most balanced option in
this price range. It offers features rarely seen at this cost, including
wireless charging, NFC for Google Pay, and a smooth 120Hz display. The
vegan leather back adds a premium touch that most budget phones lack,
making it both comfortable to hold and visually appealing.
Performance is more than enough for everyday use, handling web
browsing, streaming, messaging, and light gaming with ease. The
camera performs well in good lighting, producing solid photos suitable

OnePlus Nord N30 5G: Best Fast Charging on a
Budget
The OnePlus Nord N30 5G stands out for its charging speed. Its 50W fast
charging can fully recharge the phone in around 45 minutes, which is
significantly faster than most competitors in this price range. The large
6.72-inch Full HD+ display with a 120Hz refresh rate also delivers a
smooth and responsive experience for scrolling, gaming, and video
playback.
The 108MP main camera looks impressive on paper, but delivers mixed
results. Photos are sharp in bright outdoor settings but struggle in low-
light and indoor conditions. Software support is limited to one Android
update and three years of security patches, which feels short.
Still, for users who prioritize fast charging and a big, fluid display, the
Nord N30 5G remains a strong value.
Samsung Galaxy A25 5G: Best for Long-Term
Software Support
The Samsung Galaxy A25 5G earns its spot thanks to unmatched long-
term support. Samsung promises four years of Android OS updates and
currently offers. If longevity is your top priority, this is the safest choice in
the budget segment.
The phone features a sharp 1080p display and loud dual speakers,
making it well-suited for watching videos and listening to music.
However, multitasking can feel slow, and camera performance drops
noticeably indoors. Its plastic build and large notch also feel dated
software updates over performance or design, the Galaxy A25 5G
delivers long-term reliability.
Budget smartphones have never been better. The Moto G Power 5G
(2024) offers the best overall experience, the OnePlus Nord N30 5G
excels in charging speed and display smoothness, and the Samsung
Galaxy A25 5G leads in long-term software support.
