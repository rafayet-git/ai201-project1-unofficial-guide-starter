Google's Gemini AI takes center stage in the Pixel 9 series, offering powerful tools that go far
beyond basic assistance. You can hold full conversations with Gemini-whether you're
brainstorming for an interview, generating essay topics, or just looking for dinner ideas based
on a photo of your fridge contents. It's smart, responsive, and genuinely helpful.
Meanwhile, the Magic Editor steps in to enhance your photos by removing distractions or even
transforming dull backgrounds into something more dramatic-like replacing a gray sky with a
vivid sunset.
Gemini can generate ready-to-use email drafts, summarize long emails or documents, and
even listen in on phone calls (with the other party's consent) to provide a neat summary
afterward. That means important info like dates, appointments, or plans is always easy to
reference later.

The Pixel 9a is powered by the same Tensor G4 chip found across the entire Pixel 9 lineup. It
comes with 8GB of LPDDR5X RAM and either 128GB or 256GB of UFS 3.1 storage, though
not all regions offer both storage options. It's a bit disappointing that even the 256GB model
doesn't include a 12GB RAM configuration, especially for users who want a bit more
headroom for multitasking.
